Science Summer Camp

Whiz Kid science summer camp is a camp where Children of ages 10 and above can come and spend the summer learning about robots, space and, other science topics. This is a 6week program Starting july 1st every year and applications are open from January 1st to May 25th.

Inspiring young minds

The science field scary for alot of people to tap in, that is why for us it is so important that we show kids how fun, easy and Rewarding the science world can be.

Educating

Technology and science is the future, that is why we focus on educating young minds about different aspects of science to stimulate there minds and give them a fun understanding of simple physics and math.

Play time & Friendships

We understand that we offer a summer program, which means its a time for play dates and ice cream. That is why the Children’s schedule has hours dedicated to playing, socialising and having fun amongst other things.

Why your child should attend our summer camp!

Summer camp is all about fun, trying new things and,
making friendships that will last a lifetime. There’s something undeniably special about summer camp friendships since they last long after camp has ended.
Many times into a camper’s adult years and beyond.

Common Intrests - Kids make friends all the time, at the park, school, and during sports. But what makes summer camp friendships unique is that every camper has at least one shared interest that can help them bond. At Whiz Kid summer camp it’s safe to assume that campers are passionate about science and adventure. Sharing common interests make it easy to form a connection with campers who your child may otherwise not meet at school or through any other activity

PASSION - At Whiz Kid camp, team-building activities are intertwined with every science and adventure excursion. Sharing a moment, whether it’s launching a rocket you built together or conquering a challenging obstacle course, is an amazing way to connect.

Why we built this summer camp!

The idea for this summer camp came to me after I got children of my own and moved into a very child friendly neighbourhood. After living there for two summers I noticed how lonely and bored the children would be during the summers. Since video gaming is so popular most kids would just stay in all day playing games and miss out on all the sun because their parents worked. Because I am a scientific researcher I thought a summer cam of science might be a fun idea for kids to explore. I gathered up most of the parents to ask if that's something they would be open to and together we started Whiz Kid summer Camp.

Guided Discoveries at Whiz Kid Camp make positive differences in the lives of children.

Being away from the comforts of home does wonders for a child. They form a greater sense of independence, making them more willing to try new things, which ultimately leads them to foster their own individual character. This all occurs while developing social skills and friendships that last a lifetime without the influence of distructive technology!

Building confidence

Camp allows children to have amazingly positive experiences, and it allows them to experience setbacks and develop grit in a supportive atmosphere. There is no judgment at camp.
Whiz Kid Camp is inclusive of all individuals, resulting in a diverse community of students, campers, and staff. This exciting new place full of encouraging people provides a safe environment for them to grow and develop so they can become the best versions of themselves!
